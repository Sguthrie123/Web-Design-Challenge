Wind Speed


From the graph its clear to see we have a majority of the data between the 2.5 - 7.5 wind speed in mph. It is also clear to see that the further in each direction of the equator the wind speed rises. I think it is note worthy that the peak windspeed of the 500 places we got the weather from falls somewhere in the 50 latitude. It also worth noting that near the equator you have light winds that are known as doldrums.
