Cloudiness


As you can see at this latitude we choose the majority of the data falls on the little to none cloudiness. Now I did not know this but cloudiness is suppressed at the equator (due to equatorial ocean upwelling, hence lower SSTs, hence atmospheric subsidence). While we do have a majority of our data on the little to no clouds, I think it also worth noting that we have cloudiness also at a maximum of 100 in a few places that are near or on the equator. Thats very interesting and finding out which places those are so we can see its longitude and other details about that place would maybe help explain this phenomona.
